Investigating the application of auditory route overviews in making strategic route choices

Please read the following information carefully before you decide to take part; this will tell you why the research is being done and what you will be asked to do if you take part. Instructions

Welcome to my online study on investigating the application of auditory route overviews to make strategic route choices. The study is in two parts. The first part consists of a survey based on the usage of the auditory route overview system and the second part is an interview to discuss the experience of using the system. The study will begin by collecting demographic information, including age, gender, etc. This will be followed by a training phase, where you can interact with the system freely to practice the controls and gain familiarity with it. After the training, the survey part of the study will begin. There will be four scenarios in the survey, each followed by a couple of questions. Please take your time to understand each scenario and listen to the accompanying auditory route overview before you answer the questions. You can interact with the audio by pausing, stopping, restarting, rewinding, forward-winding, and muting different types of information. You can interact with the audio as you wish to enable you to answer the questions.


During the survey part of the study, only your key-strokes and interactions with the system will be recorded. The interview part will only be audio recorded. No video recordings will be made.

It is up to you to decide whether or not to take part. If you do decide to take part you will be given this information sheet to keep and be asked to sign a consent form.
