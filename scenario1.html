Scenario 1 of 4

Welcome to the study scenarios. Just a reminder, there are four scenarios in all. Each scenario is made up of several sub-scenarios. You are encouraged to take breaks during the study at the end of any subsceraio. You should leave the browser window open, and just come back to resume.

Purpose

The first scenario is designed to investigate whether the auditory route overviews are helpful in making decisions regarding route choices.

There are three sub-scenarios in this scenario. For each of them, you have to choose a route for going from place X to place Y, from a list of auditory route overviews given. This is followed by some questions regarding the choices you have made.

Reminder: You can interact freely with the audio routes: you can pause, unpause, rewind, forward, bookmark, jump to bookmark and mute the individual sound sources. You can also replay the audio as many times as you wish. Please bear in mind that there are no correct or incorrect answer.
