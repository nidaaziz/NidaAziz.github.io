Street Safety Survey



A warm hello to you. At WalkTogether it is our vision to create co-designed products for womxn (/wʊmən/)[?] to enhance their street safety. We believe in utilising the unique experiences, ideas and preferences of all womxn to design tech products that are more useful and usable for them.


Through this survey we want to investigate whether womxn feel safe or not while walking on the street and what can be done to improve it. The survey has 21 questions in all and should take around 10-15 minutes of your time. It will provide us with information to start creating a positive impact. All your data will be completely anonymised. In the spirit of codesign, we kindly request you to respond genuinely and generously. Thank you so much.


If you would like to learn more about our work or discuss further, please email at info.buddyup@gmail.com
